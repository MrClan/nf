About Us

The Health & Research Hub is a leading government organization committed to improving public health and advancing scientific knowledge. Established with a mandate to serve all citizens, we conduct pioneering research, develop evidence-based health policies, and implement innovative programs that empower individuals to make informed health decisions. Our mission is to bridge the gap between research and practice, fostering collaborative partnerships that drive meaningful change.

We work closely with healthcare professionals, academic institutions, and community organizations to address pressing health challenges. Our initiatives span a wide range of areas, including disease prevention, chronic disease management, mental health support, and health promotion.

Our Research Initiatives

The NHRC is responsible for conducting and supporting research related to health within the Republic of Nepal, subject to the health policy of the Government of Nepal. We focus on impactful research that contributes significantly to global health advancements. We focus on critical areas such as infectious diseases, non-communicable diseases, environmental health impacts, and the application of cutting-edge technologies like artificial intelligence in diagnostics. Our findings inform public health strategies and contribute to building a healthier, more resilient society.

Infectious Disease Control

Developing strategies for preventing and managing infectious diseases, including emerging pathogens.

Chronic Disease Management

Research focused on improving the quality of life for people with chronic conditions like heart disease, diabetes, and cancer.

Environmental Health

Studying the impact of environmental factors on public health and promoting protective interventions for cleaner environments.

Digital Health Technologies

Exploring the use of digital tools and artificial intelligence to enhance healthcare delivery and patient outcomes.
