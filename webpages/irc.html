Institutional Review Committee (IRC)

Responsibilities, submission guidelines, and workflow for institutional-level ethical review.

View Workflow

Responsibilities

Submission Guide

Ethical Oversight

Ensure protocols meet ethical standards, protect participants, and comply with NHRC policies.

Local Governance

Coordinate with host institutions to maintain review records, approvals, and continuing review.

Capacity Building

Support training on consent, data protection, safety reporting, and community engagement.

Submission Guidelines

Forms
Completed protocol, informed consent forms, participant materials, data plan.
Timelines
Standard review timelines and expedited criteria based on risk level.
Reporting
Adverse events, protocol deviations, and annual continuing review submissions.

Workflow Diagram (Text Summary)

Stepwise Flow

1) Submission → 2) Administrative check → 3) Reviewer assignment → 4) Primary review → 5) Committee decision → 6) Communication of outcome → 7) Continuing review/monitoring.
