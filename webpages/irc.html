Responsibilities

  • Ethical oversight of local protocols; participant protections.
  • Maintain records of approvals, amendments, and continuing review.
  • Coordinate with investigators on consent, safety, and data practices.
  • Strengthen institutional capacity through guidance and training.

Submission Checklist

  • Protocol + objectives + methodology
  • Consent/assent forms; participant materials
  • Data plan (privacy, storage, sharing)
  • Safety reporting and monitoring approach
  • Investigator CVs and roles

Workflow (Text Summary)

1) Submission

Protocol and required documents submitted to IRC.

2) Admin Check

Completeness review; eligibility for expedited vs full.

3) Reviewer Assignment

Assign primary/secondary reviewers based on expertise.

4) Review

Risk-benefit, consent, data protection, vulnerable groups.

5) Decision

Approve / conditions / modify / reject; minutes recorded.

6) Communicate

Outcome letter and required actions to PI.

7) Continuing Review

Renewals, amendments, safety reports, site monitoring.
