Institutional Research Ethics Review Process (IRERP)

System overview for managing ethics submissions, reviews, and continuing oversight.

Key Features

System Highlights

Digital Submissions

Online intake of protocols, consent forms, and supporting documents with completeness checks.

Tracking & Notifications

Status tracking, reviewer assignments, and alerts for actions and deadlines.

Continuing Review

Manage amendments, adverse event reporting, and annual renewals within the system.

Access & Support

Access
Available to registered institutions and investigators; contact NHRC for onboarding.
Helpdesk
Technical and process support for submissions, updates, and reporting.
Security
Data protection aligned with NHRC policies and participant confidentiality requirements.
