Ethical Review Process

Step-by-step flow, required documents, and approval stages for NHRC ethics review.

See Steps

Process Steps

Flow

1) Submission (protocol, consent, tools) → 2) Admin completeness check → 3) Reviewer assignment → 4) Ethics review (risk-benefit, consent, data protection) → 5) Committee decision (approve/conditions/modify/reject) → 6) Communication to PI → 7) Continuing review and reporting.

Required Documents

Protocol
Full protocol with objectives, methodology, risk mitigation, and data plan.
Consent
Informed consent/assent forms in relevant languages, including data use and withdrawal rights.
Materials
Questionnaires, recruitment materials, compensation details, and safety monitoring plan.

Approval Stages

Full Board

For higher-risk studies; discussed in convened meetings.

Expedited

Minimal risk studies may undergo expedited review by designated reviewers.

Exemption

Specific low-risk categories may be exempt, with documentation retained by NHRC.
