Process Steps

1) Submission

Submit protocol, consent, participant materials, and data/safety plans.

2) Admin Check

Completeness screening; routing to expedited or full review track.

3) Reviewer Assignment

Primary/secondary reviewers matched to topic and methodology.

4) Ethics Review

Risk-benefit, consent quality, data protection, vulnerable populations.

5) Decision

Approve, approve with conditions, request modification, or reject.

6) Communicate

Decision letter with conditions and timelines sent to PI.

7) Continuing Review

Renewals, amendments, safety reporting, site monitoring.

Required Documents

  • Protocol: objectives, methodology, risk mitigation, data plan
  • Consent/assent forms; multilingual where relevant
  • Recruitment and participant materials
  • Compensation details and safety monitoring plan
  • Investigator qualifications and roles

Approval Stages

Full Board

Higher-risk studies discussed in convened meetings with quorum.

Expedited

Minimal-risk protocols reviewed by designated reviewers.

Exempt

Specific low-risk categories documented and archived by NHRC.
