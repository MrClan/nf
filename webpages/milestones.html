Timeline

2025 — Telehealth Expansion

Launched nationwide telehealth pilot integrating rural clinics with provincial hospitals for specialist consultations.

Year: 2025 Impact: 30+ clinics

2024 — Ethics Review Modernization

Digitized protocol submissions and tracking, reducing review cycle time and improving transparency.

Year: 2024 Impact: Faster reviews

2023 — Data Governance Framework

Established national standards for health research data stewardship, privacy, and sharing.

Year: 2023 Impact: Data quality

2021 — Community Research Network

Built a community partner network enabling rapid field studies and inclusive participant recruitment.

Year: 2021 Impact: National reach
