Ethical Framework

Review Process

Respect for Persons

Dignity, autonomy, and informed consent at every interaction.

Beneficence

Maximize benefit, minimize risk, and safeguard participant welfare.

Justice

Fair selection and equitable distribution of research benefits.

Accountability

Transparent governance, COI management, and ongoing compliance.

Organizational Responsibility

Governance

ERB/IRC oversight, protocol review, conditions, and continuing review.

Capacity

Training on consent, data protection, safety reporting, community engagement.

Transparency

Clear communication of decisions, amendments, and reporting obligations.
