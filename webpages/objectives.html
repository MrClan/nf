Our Objectives

Clear, measurable goals that guide NHRC’s mission to advance health research, ethics, and innovation across Nepal.

View Goals

Key Goals & Objectives

Download Charter

Advance Evidence-Based Policy

Generate and synthesize research evidence that informs national health policy and program design.

Strengthen Research Ethics

Ensure all health research aligns with international ethical standards and protects participant rights.

Build Research Capacity

Provide training, mentorship, and resources to cultivate skilled researchers across Nepal.

Foster Innovation

Promote adoption of digital health tools and emerging technologies to improve care delivery.

Expand Collaboration

Partner with academia, industry, and communities to co-create impactful health solutions.

Enhance Data Stewardship

Improve data quality, security, and accessibility to accelerate research and public health response.
