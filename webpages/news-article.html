Telehealth Expansion Reaches Rural Communities

Published: Jan 8, 2025

Article

NHRC has partnered with provincial hospitals to introduce secure telehealth services in rural municipalities. Early pilot data shows faster triage, reduced travel burden for patients, and improved clinical documentation.

Over the next quarter, the program will expand to 30 additional clinics, focusing on maternal health, chronic disease management, and emergency consultations. Training modules for clinicians emphasize privacy, ethics, and quality assurance.

Evaluation metrics include patient satisfaction, referral turnaround time, and cost savings. Findings will inform national policy recommendations and guide future scale-up.
