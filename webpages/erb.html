FERCAP Recognition (since 2019): NHRC ERB is recognized for meeting international standards in human subject protection and ethics oversight.

Overview

Mandate

Ethical oversight for protocols reviewed by NHRC; protect rights, safety, and data integrity.

Scope

Clinical trials, public health studies, observational designs, data-driven research with human participants.

Standards

Aligned to international ethics norms and national regulations; continuous quality improvement.

Key Functions

Review

Risk-benefit assessment, consent evaluation, protections for vulnerable groups.

Oversight

Continuing review, amendments, safety reporting, data monitoring triggers.

Guidance

Support investigators with ethics queries, templates, and training opportunities.

Recognition Timeline

2019

FERCAP recognition awarded for human subject protection standards.

2021

Process updates for digital submissions and tracking.

2024

Expanded continuing review guidance and safety reporting refinements.
