Ethical Review Board (ERB)

FERCAP recognized since 2019; safeguarding participant rights, safety, and scientific integrity.

See Functions

Overview & Recognition

Functions

Mandate

Provide ethical oversight for health research proposals reviewed by NHRC.

FERCAP Recognition

Recognized by FERCAP since 2019 for adhering to international standards of human subject protection.

Scope

Clinical trials, public health studies, observational research, and data-driven projects with human participants.

Functions

Review
Protocol review, risk-benefit assessment, consent process evaluation.
Oversight
Continuing review, amendments, safety reporting, data monitoring triggers.
Guidance
Ethics guidance to investigators and institutions; training and capacity building.
