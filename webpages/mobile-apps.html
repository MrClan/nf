Support & FAQ

How do I request access?

Use the “Get App” link and follow onboarding prompts; institutional accounts can be requested via NHRC.

Are apps available offline?

Most features support offline data entry with sync when connectivity returns.

Which languages are supported?

English and Nepali for core apps; see comparison table for specifics.
